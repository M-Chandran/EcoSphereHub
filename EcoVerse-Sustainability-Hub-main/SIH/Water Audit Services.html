Frequently Asked Questions

What is mine rehabilitation?

Mine rehabilitation is the process of restoring mined land to a natural or economically usable state after mining activities have ceased.

Why is carbon offset certification important?

Carbon offset certification helps ensure that projects designed to reduce greenhouse gas emissions are effective and meet established standards.
