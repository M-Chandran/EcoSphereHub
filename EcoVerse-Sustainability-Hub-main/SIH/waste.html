Waste Segregation Guidelines

Proper waste segregation is crucial for effective recycling and waste management. Follow these guidelines:

  • Separate organic waste (e.g., food scraps, yard waste) from recyclable waste.
  • Dispose of plastic, paper, metal, and electronic waste in designated bins.
  • Do not mix hazardous waste with regular waste.
  • Check local regulations for specific waste disposal guidelines.
